Information Stored Locally

The apps store quiz progress, selected answers, incorrect answer history, bookmarked review items, language preference, theme preference, timer preference, and similar learning settings on the user's device. This local learning data is used only to operate the study experience.

Purchases and Entitlements

The apps may use Google Play Billing and RevenueCat to process purchases, verify the lifetime access entitlement, and restore purchases. The apps do not receive or store credit card numbers, bank account details, or full payment credentials. Payment processing is handled by Google Play and related purchase infrastructure.

Data Collection and Sharing

The apps do not require user accounts and do not collect names, addresses, phone numbers, contacts, location, photos, messages, health data, or government identifiers. Purchase history and entitlement status may be processed by Google Play Billing and RevenueCat as service providers for app functionality, purchase validation, restore purchase support, fraud prevention, and basic purchase analytics.

Advertising

The apps do not display third-party ads and do not use advertising ID for ad targeting.

Children

The apps are intended for adult certification learners and are not directed to children.

Retention and Deletion

Local learning data remains on the user's device until the user resets learning data in the app, clears app storage, or uninstalls the app. Purchase records may be retained by Google Play, RevenueCat, or related service providers according to their own legal, fraud-prevention, and operational requirements.

Security

Purchase and entitlement checks are transmitted through provider-managed secure connections. No method of transmission or storage is perfect, but the apps are designed to avoid collecting unnecessary personal data.

Changes

This policy may be updated when app functionality, store requirements, or service provider practices change. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

Privacy inquiries can be submitted through the developer contact channel shown on the Google Play store listing for the relevant app.
